About

I'm Blackbird — an anonymous maker of small browser games and toys. I like ideas that fit in a single weekend and run with zero installs.

What I'm about

Most of my projects start as a "what if" — what if time only moved when you did, what if your gun was also your engine, what if a puzzle grew like a garden. I build the smallest version that answers the question, ship it to the browser, and move on to the next one. No frameworks unless a game truly needs them; a lot of it is just canvas and plain JavaScript.

When something turns out to be more fun than expected, it graduates to the games page. Everything there is playable right now, in this tab, on desktop or phone.
