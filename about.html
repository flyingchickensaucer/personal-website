About

Hey.

I'm Blackbird. I use a handle instead of my name — I like keeping the work and the person a little separate.

I've been building for the web for a while now. Mostly small games, sometimes a tool I wanted and couldn't find, occasionally something I made just to see if it would work. I stick to plain HTML, canvas, and JavaScript where I can, and only reach for a framework when a project actually needs one.

None of it is trying to be a product or a startup. It's a place to put the things I'm glad I finished. Right now I'm tidying that up, so there's not much to see yet.
