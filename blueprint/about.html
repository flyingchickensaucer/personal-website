Field Notes · About Me

Hey.

Hi, I'm Blackbird. On this side of the site we have my actual namesake, the SR-71 Blackbird.

I've been building whatever for a while now. Mostly small games, sometimes a tool I wanted and couldn't find, once in a while something I made just to see whether it would work. I'm actually much more of a hardware person, believe it or not. Maybe I'll put some of that up here at some point.

As for things I like, as you can guess from the UI, I like space. And air. And aerospace. And astrophysics. As such, I dabble in amateur rocketery once in a while. Drones too, those are cool. As for hobbies, I love to tell others I enjoy hiking and reading, but in reality I'm a chronic doomscroller. I also rather enjoy video games, especially FPS, but I'm also rather bad at those. Who knows, maybe one day this page will be a little longer, but for now, this is it I guess.
